Help & FAQs

1. How does the converter work?

It listens to your voice or processes an uploaded audio file, converts it into text, and then displays corresponding sign language animations.

2. How to use it?

  • Click on Start Listening and speak into your mic.
  • Or upload a supported audio file (MP3, WAV).
  • The system will show corresponding sign language gestures.

3. What if animation is not appearing?

Please check your mic access, ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome/Edge), and verify audio clarity.
