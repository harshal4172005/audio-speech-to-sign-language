Help & FAQs

1. How does the converter work?

It listens to your voice or processes uploaded audio, converts it into text, then displays animated sign language for the words.

2. How to use it?
  • Click Start Listening and speak clearly.
  • Or upload an audio file (.mp3/.wav).
  • Sign language gestures will appear instantly.
3. What if animations don't show?

Ensure your microphone is allowed, use Chrome/Edge browser, and verify that you're speaking clearly into a working mic.

4. Can I share feedback?

Yes! Please share your suggestions at SLCconverter01@gmail.com.
